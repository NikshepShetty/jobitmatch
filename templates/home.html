Welcome to jobITmatch!

Have you ever been able to pinpoint what you personally like in IT?

Perhaps you're tired of the current job you're working at, because it just doesn't feel right for you.

The overwhelming amount of information that the internet provides about the IT sector can be confusing and scary, and can demotivate anyone to research further.

That's exactly what we attempt to provide.

JobITmatch provides a resource in understanding the various job profiles in IT. We aim to educate you about the descriptions of the Job Profiles currently in the IT sector and the recommended skills required to get hired for a job in the position.

JobITmatch also lets you assess your skills, and recommends job profiles according to your Personality and Resume.

O*Net

O*NET is our websites source for occupational information. Valid data is essential to understanding the rapidly changing nature of work and how it impacts the workforce, and so O*Net serves as a medium to deliver this.

The O*NET database, contains hundreds of standardized and occupation-specific descriptors on almost 1,000 occupations covering the entire workforce. We on our website have primarily only focussed on IT job profiles, but O*Net provides descriptions on overy job profile imaginable.
